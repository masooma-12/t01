About Us

Mikasa’s Journal – The Story of Mikasa Café After Eren’s death, I felt lost. The world we fought for finally existed, yet my heart remained trapped in the past. I couldn’t keep living in sorrow—I knew that wasn’t what he would have wanted. So, I chose to build something new, something warm. **Mikasa Café** wasn’t just a business; it was a way to honor him, to honor all of them. Here, in the heart of Shiganshina, my home, I found purpose again. The mornings are filled with the comforting scent of fresh bread and roasted coffee. The soft hum of conversation and laughter now replaces the echoes of battle. Armin helps with the books, always lost in thought, sketching new ideas between orders. Jean complains about being overworked but never actually leaves the counter. Connie and Sasha’s recipes live on, their flavors bringing joy to those who stop by. Even Levi visits sometimes, though he insists he’s only here for the tea. Every day, I wake up knowing this place is more than just a café—it’s a promise that we survived, that we’re still moving forward. And even though Eren is gone, a part of him will always be here, in the warmth of the sunrise through the windows, in the quiet strength that keeps me going. This is home. This is Mikasa Café.
